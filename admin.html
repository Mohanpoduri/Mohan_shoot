How It Works

Your photos are stored as base64 data in the browser's localStorage and served directly from there. No external server needed — just deploy your two HTML files anywhere for free.

📁 Storage: Browser localStorage (no database needed)
🔒 Admin: Password protected panel
🌐 Hosting: Any static host — Netlify, GitHub Pages, Vercel
🔗 Share: Just share your site URL in instabio

Deploy Your Site — Step by Step

Follow these steps to get your portfolio live for free in under 10 minutes:

1
Download both files: index.html and admin.html from this page
2
Go to netlify.com → Sign up free with your Google account
3
Click Sites → Add new site → Deploy manually
4
Drag & drop both index.html and admin.html into the deploy box
5
Netlify gives you a free URL like your-name.netlify.app
6
Visit your-name.netlify.app/admin.html → upload your photos
7
Share your-name.netlify.app in your instabio link!

⚠ Important: Photos you upload from admin.html will be saved in that browser only. To see them on another device, upload from the deployed Netlify URL, not locally.
